Solicitor's Permit Application

Profit and non-profit organizations, as well as individuals, are permitted to conduct door to door sales in residential neighborhoods with an approved Reserve Twp Police Department Solicitor's Permit.

Prior to initiating any sales, a Solicitor's Permit Application must be completed and approved by the Reserve Twp Police Department. Profit and Non-profit organizations are required to register each and every individual that will be selling door to door on behalf of the organization. No sales are permitted to begin until the application has been approved and a sales permit identification has been issued by the Police Department.
